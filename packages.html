Our Packages

School Alliance is a grand program through which we plant the seed of engineering in the mind of school going students and sparks the light of working practically and think in more dimensions.

It’s a 12 month long association with the school. The complete course outline is designed in such a way that it covers all the science topics according to each grade level. It is comprised of three different levels for different grades:

Little Leader

The Little Leaders program is a base program in which the primary grade students get a golden opportunity to become young scientist, engineers, mathematician and leaders. Kids work closely together, using hands-on methods to solve real-world problems. We provide students enough room to kick off their journey toward becoming creative, innovative, critical thinkers.

Course Objective

Becoming familiar with circuit boards, electronic components, Arduino board to learn the basics of electronics and computer science 1.0 to solve real life problems.

Age Group

This package introduces kids to the wondrous world of Electronics and Engineering. It is suitable for kids who are just beginning to understand how the world works. Grade 3-6

Class size

No more than 25 students are permitted in each class to maintain quality attention by trainers.

Sessions/ Schedule

-Two classes in a week ( One hour each)
- One class in a week (Two hours)

STEM Concepts learned

Science: motion, energy, electricity, temperature, sound, waves, colors, force
Technology: mechanical designing, programming, software basics
Engineering: sensors, electronic components, robotics
Maths: distance measurements, angles, quick maths

Let the future leader decide

Let your child try our first session for free so they can decide if it is interesting for them. We are trying to make them future leaders and the practice starts from day one.

Inventors

The Inventors program is a detailed program through which we spark the light of technology in the child, so that the child starts thinking and learning science in a much better way. The child tends to learn the electronic Science behind the Robots, the Technology behind the hardware mechanicals, the software engineering to make the hardware live, and the mathematical algorithms of different sensors.

Course Objective

Each level comprises of 30 lectures that help the student to learn from the scratch till the peak of perfection in STEM subjects. Inspires creativity and critical thinking. Brainstorm ideas and attempt to implement them Students discover the engineering world.

Sessions/ Schedule

-Two classes in a week ( One hour each)
- One class in a week (Two hours)

STEM Concepts learned

Science: communication, acceleration, speed, direction, weight, force, power, energy
Technology: infrared, ultrasonic waves, proximity sensors, programming, GUI, mobile apps
Engineering: robotics, electronic components, controllers, arduino, circuit designing
Maths: distance measurements, measurement of angles, quick maths, geometric solutions

MasterMinds

Masterminds is an advance program through which we plant the seed of engineering in the mind of school going students and sparks the light of working practically and think in more dimensions.
